Frequently Asked Questions

Is this an online image converter that stores my files?

No! RealMultiTool is built using JavaScript. All conversions happen locally in your web browser. Your files never touch our servers, guaranteeing absolute privacy.

Does this work as a transparent image converter?

Yes. If you convert a transparent PNG, GIF, or SVG into another PNG or WEBP, the transparency is perfectly preserved. If you convert to JPG (which doesn't support transparency), the background defaults to a clean white.

Can I use this as a 4k image converter?

Absolutely. RealMultiTool reads the exact dimensions of your uploaded file. If you upload a 4k resolution image, it will maintain that high quality during the conversion process.

Is there a file size limit for uploads?

Since the processing is done locally on your device, there is no strict file size limit imposed by a server. Your only limit is the available memory (RAM) on your computer or mobile device.

Do I need to install any software or extensions?

No installation is required. RealMultiTool runs entirely within your web browser, making it completely safe and immediately accessible without downloading third-party apps.

Will there be a watermark on my converted images?

Absolutely not. We believe your images belong to you. All conversions are 100% watermark-free, preserving your original artwork and professional graphics.

Does this tool work on mobile phones and tablets?

Yes! RealMultiTool is fully responsive. You can convert images on your smartphone or tablet just as easily as on a desktop computer, directly from your mobile browser.

What happens if I lose my internet connection?

Because RealMultiTool relies on HTML5 and JavaScript to process images locally in your browser, you can actually continue to convert files even if your internet connection drops after loading the page!

Is the tool completely free to use?

Yes, RealMultiTool is 100% free. There are no hidden fees, no premium subscriptions, and no daily usage limits.

Does converting an image reduce its quality?

Our tool uses optimal conversion algorithms to ensure minimal visual loss when converting to formats like JPG or WEBP. Formats like PNG remain mathematically lossless.

Can I convert multiple images at once?

Currently, our tool is optimized for lightning-fast, single-file conversions to guarantee stability across all devices. Batch processing is a feature we are exploring for future updates.

Are my converted files backed up or saved anywhere?

No. For your privacy and security, we do not have access to your files. Once you refresh the page or close the tab, the uploaded data is permanently cleared from your device's temporary memory.

Why is this converter faster than other online tools?

Traditional converters require you to upload your file to a server, wait in a queue, and then download it back. RealMultiTool eliminates upload and download wait times by doing the processing right on your device.
