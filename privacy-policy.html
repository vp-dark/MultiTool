Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 8, 2026

Welcome to MultiTool ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use our website (multitool.com) and our suite of free online utilities.

1. How We Process Your Files (Client-Side Processing)

MultiTool is designed with your privacy as the highest priority. Our Image Resizer, File Converter, and other core tools operate entirely on the "client-side." This means that when you upload an image or file, it is processed directly by your web browser on your own device.

  • We do not upload your files to our servers.
  • We do not store, view, or analyze your files.
  • Once you close your browser or refresh the page, all processed data is completely cleared from your active session.

2. Google AdSense and Cookies

To keep MultiTool free for everyone, we use third-party advertising companies, specifically Google AdSense, to serve ads when you visit our website.

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. (Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info).

3. General Website Analytics

We may use basic, anonymized web analytics to understand how many people visit our site and which tools are the most popular. This data includes general information like browser type, device type, and referring pages. It does not include personally identifiable information or the contents of any files you process.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
